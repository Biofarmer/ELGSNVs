Description:

SNV_code_Figure 1: SNV profiling and quality control. This script merges and processes the inStrain SNV, genome, scaffold, and gene-level outputs and applies the main SNV filtering and quality-control procedures. It visualizes genome-wide SNV density, sequencing coverage, nucleotide diversity, SNV burden, mutation types, variant classes, and allele-frequency distributions.

SNV_code_Figure 2: Developmental trajectories of microbial SNVs over age. This script quantifies age-related changes in microbial SNV rate and nucleotide diversity from birth to 3 years of age. It uses generalized additive models and derivative analyses to identify developmental transition points and summarizes age-associated SNV patterns at the species and gene levels.

SNV_code_Figure 3: Relationships among SNV rate, abundance and prevalence. This script calculates species SNV burden, SNV rate, relative abundance, prevalence, and the N/S ratio. It evaluates relationships among these metrics and compares SNV rate distributions across species-prevalence groups.

SNV_code_Figure 4: SNV-defined community states. This script applies Dirichlet multinomial mixture clustering to identify SNV-defined community states and select the optimal number of clusters. It characterizes cluster-specific SNV features, their developmental distributions, and their differences based on microbial SNV rate.

SNV_code_Figure 5: Host and environmental associations. This script constructs a sample-by-species SNV rate matrix and calculates pairwise Euclidean distances between samples. It evaluates associations of host and environmental factors with microbial SNVs and SNV-defined community states using Mantel tests, regression models, and recursive partitioning analysis.

SNV_code_Figure 6: Prematurity-associated microbial SNV variation. This script compares microbial SNV rate, nucleotide diversity, and their age-dependent trajectories between preterm and full-term infants. It identifies prematurity-associated species- and gene-level SNV rate differences and characterizes differences in SNV-defined community states distributions according to term and NEC status.

SNV_code_Figure 7: NEC-associated SNVs and candidate variants. This script analyzes longitudinal microbial SNV patterns across noNEC, preNEC, and postNEC samples and identifies nominal preNEC-enriched candidate SNV variants. It prioritizes candidate species, genomes, genes, and linked SNV modules for downstream functional annotation and protein-structure visualization.
